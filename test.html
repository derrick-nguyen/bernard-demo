Our B2B AI Copilot helps businesses reduce their carbon footprint. It's like having an expert analyst, data scientist, and consultant on your team.

How it works

Machine learning algorithms

Our AI uses machine learning to analyze complex data patterns and make predictions for energy use and emissions.

Data analysis

Our AI analyzes large volumes of data, such as energy consumption and production, to identify trends and insights.

Real-time data analysis

Our AI can analyze data in real time, providing instant feedback and insights for optimizing energy use and reducing emissions.

IoT integration

Our AI can integrate with IoT (Internet of Things) devices, such as smart sensors and meters, to collect real-time data for energy use and emissions.

Predictive analytics

Our AI can use historical data, real-time sensor data, and external factors to predict future energy use and emissions, helping businesses plan and optimize their operations.

Energy management system

Our AI can provide recommendations for energy efficiency measures, renewable energy sources, emission reduction strategies, and other actions to help businesses reduce their carbon footprint.

Renewable energy recommendation

Eco-friendly supply chain optimization
